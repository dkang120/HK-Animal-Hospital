Our Story & Mission

Dr. Tae Woo Kang practiced veterinary medicine for many years as part of a corporate practice. He opened HK Animal Hospital to fulfill his lifelong dream of providing excellent, personalized care for each and every patient.

Our Faith & Values

"As a Christian veterinary hospital, we will share God’s compassion and grace with our employees, neighbors, clients, and the community."

We believe that gentle care comes from gentle and professional staff and a clean and welcoming working environment. Dr. Kang is committed to using his experience and knowledge of advanced veterinary medicine to provide the highest quality veterinary care in the most cost-efficient manner possible.
